Adventures in Fire Island

My recent trip to Fire Island was an unforgettable escape into nature. From the moment I stepped off the ferry, I was greeted with pristine beaches, rolling dunes, and the soothing sound of the Atlantic surf. Highlights included hiking through the Sunken Forest, spotting deer along the trails, and watching the sun set over the bay. Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat or an active outdoor adventure, Fire Island has something for everyone.
